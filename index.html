About me

Who I am

Hi! I'm Nico, a Roblox developer who loves taking an idea from a blank baseplate to a finished, playable game. I care about code that's readable, modular, and built to scale.

My focus is scripting and game systems — combat, data saving, economies, UI logic — using a server-authoritative approach so games stay fair and exploit-resistant. I'm comfortable jumping into an existing project or starting one from zero.

I'm always learning and open to commissions, collabs, and long-term team work. If you've got a project, let's talk.
